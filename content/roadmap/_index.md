    <div class="section__header">
      <h1>Roadmap</h1>
      <p>Planned milestones and the direction of travel for OpenRoadBook. This page outlines short-, mid-, and long-term goals so contributors and users can follow progress.</p>
    </div>

    <div class="resource-grid">
      <article class="resource-card">
        <h3>Short term (0-6 months)</h3>
        <ul>
          <li>Stabilize schema and improve validator feedback messages.</li>
          <li>Publish printable templates and export examples.</li>
          <li>Expand the symbol registry with localization and clearer IDs.</li>
        </ul>
      </article>

      <article class="resource-card">
        <h3>Medium term (6-18 months)</h3>
        <ul>
          <li>Introduce FIA-compatible profiles and higher-assurance metadata.</li>
          <li>Build scrutineering guidelines and tooling integrations.</li>
          <li>Provide telemetry and dashboard prototypes for partners.</li>
        </ul>
      </article>

      <article class="resource-card">
        <h3>Long term (18+ months)</h3>
        <ul>
          <li>Partner integrations and stable pro-rally extensions.</li>
          <li>Official certification profiles and compliance tooling.</li>
          <li>Expanded platform integrations and community governance.</li>
        </ul>
      </article>
    </div>
  </div>
