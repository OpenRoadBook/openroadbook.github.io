# Roadmap

Planned milestones and the direction of travel for OpenRoadBook. This page outlines short-, mid-, and long-term goals so contributors and users can follow progress.

## Short term (0-6 months)

- Stabilize schema and improve validator feedback messages.
- Publish printable templates and export examples.
- Expand the symbol registry with localization and clearer IDs.

## Medium term (6-18 months)

- Introduce FIA-compatible profiles and higher-assurance metadata.
- Build scrutineering guidelines and tooling integrations.
- Provide telemetry and dashboard prototypes for partners.

## Long term (18+ months)

- Partner integrations and stable pro-rally extensions.
- Official certification profiles and compliance tooling.
- Expanded platform integrations and community governance.
